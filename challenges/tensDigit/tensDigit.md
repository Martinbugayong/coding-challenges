## tensDigit

**Difficulty**: basic 

## Prompt 

The `tensDigit` function should	take as input an integer and output an integer equal to the 
tens digit of its input. We think of numbers less than 10 as having a zero in the tens digit.

## Examples

```js 
tensDigit(123) //=> 2
tensDigit(5) //=> 0
tensDigit(4561) //=> 6
```
